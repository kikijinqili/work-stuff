HLA Genotyping Kits

The human leukocyte antigen (HLA) complex is a gene complex located on the short arm of human chromosome 6. The human version of the major histocompatibility complex (MHC) allows the immune system to distinguish the host cells from invaders. It is the most polymorphic locus in the human genome, and has been implicated in many pathologies including autoimmunity disorders and cancer.
